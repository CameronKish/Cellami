Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Data Collection

By default, Cellami ("the Add-in") connects directly to a complementary application running locally on your device ("Cellami desktop application"), and all AI processing is performed locally. The Add-in does not transmit your Excel data to external cloud servers owned by the publisher.

2. Third-Party AI Providers

If you choose to enable optional cloud-based features (such as "Turbo Mode" or external API connections), your data may be transmitted directly to the configured third-party provider for processing. The publisher does not have access to this data, and its handling is subject to the privacy policy of the respective third-party provider.

3. Local Data

Data stored by the Cellami desktop application (such as Knowledge Base files and Chat History) is persisted only on your local hard drive. You maintain full control and ownership of this data.

4. Telemetry

The Add-in may collect anonymous technical telemetry (such as crash reports or usage statistics) to improve stability. This telemetry does not contain personal information or user content.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our support channel.
